Summary: Latitude vs Weather Variables

Visualizations

Approximately 500 cities of varying latitudes were chosen at random. The data was gathered through the Google OpenWeatherMap API and then analytics were applied to create visualizations to illustrate the certain relationships. Maximum/minimum Temperatures, Cloudiness, Wind Speed, and Humidity were major areas of statistical interest. Using Matplotlib, different visualizations were generated which attempted to reconcile any possible relationship between the various weather factors and latitude. Each visualization is also paired with its own analysis of the data and lists observable trends.
